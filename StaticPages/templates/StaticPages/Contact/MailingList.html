Mailing Lists

CUCC Communicate primarily via email. The best way of staying up to date with the goings-on of the CUCC is to sign up to the mailing list (caving-main@srcf.net). This link requires a Raven login to access. If you are outside of the university and do not have a Raven login, please contact the President or the Secretary, who will be able to add your email address to the list.

Due to the amount of spam sent to these lists, all emails not from a list member or an @cam.ac.uk address are silently discarded.

Alternatively, if you want to get in contact with the club, you can find addresses for the rest of the committee here or send a message to our facebook page.

Other mailing lists

The committee mailing list is here (caving-admin@srcf.net), and the expedition mailing list is here (expo@lists.wookware.org). Please follow these links to sign up.
