Bolt placement

   4 Lump Hammers 

   7 Bolting Hammers 

   9 Hilti Setters 

   6 Hand bolt setters 

   Lots of Spits, cones and hiltis (Worth checking if we need more, perhaps with Olly Madge) 

   16 Plastic pots fpr carrying spits etc.

Rigging gear

   8 Tapes 

   54 Maillons 

   21 Clown hangers 

   42 Twist Hangers

   51 Bend hangers

   2 AS Hangers

   8 Rope protectors

   3 Drill Bits

Anthony says: this doesn't sound like very many drill bits - they have a habit of getting dropped down impenetrable cracks or disappearing into the depths of peoples personal gear. I would suggest getting at least a couple more unless there are some more in Cambridge.
Serena says: Olly M will buy three extra drill bits along with a box of 100 of Hilti HKD (Hilti product code 376959). 

   1 13mm spanner

   20 Through bolts - Note these probably do not last as long under ground due to the  

   thin metal in their collar

Serena says: Martin will have words with everybody to the effect of avoiding this mistake again. They were only used for bolting up, and if the route goes they should be substituted as soon as possible.

Surveying gear

   13 Nail Varmish

   13 Therion protractors

   9 Pencils

Anthony says: this is DEFINITELY not enough pencils. We always go through more pencils than anyone expects as they regularly get lost and broken - I would be inclined to make sure we start expo with at least 50 pencils available.
Serena says: Emma bought 50 pencils. 

   9 Notebooks

Anthony says: do we have any idea what sort of paper these are made of, whether they are squared paper etc? With the 10 new books we have as sponsorship, and an implication that there are some more at basecamp, we will probably be ok. 

   2 surveying clipboards

Anthony says: this doesn't sound like many clipboards - are there any more hiding somewhere?
Serena says: Wookey says that plenty were fettled last year. Where they are is still unknown to me.
